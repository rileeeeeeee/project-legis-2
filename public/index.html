How Project Legis Works

1

Input Your Legal Text

Paste any legal clause into the text area. Select the clause type to provide context for more accurate analysis. Our system handles everything from short clauses to multi-page agreements.

2

AI-Powered Benchmarking

Project Legis uses Google's Gemini 1.5 Flash model to analyze your text against thousands of market-standard agreements. Our specialized prompt engineering extracts key insights about strengths, weaknesses, and negotiation points.

3

Interactive Analysis

The system presents a comprehensive market analysis along with your original document. For deeper examination, highlight any text and click "Analyze Selection" to get context-specific insights about that particular clause or phrase.

About Project Legis

Project Legis was born from a recognition that while AI won't replace lawyers, it will fundamentally transform how legal professionals work. The next generation of lawyers won't be replaced by AI‚Äîthey'll be professionals who know how to leverage AI to enhance their practice.

As an experimental project, Legis explores how AI can help:

  • Quickly benchmark contracts against market standards
  • Identify potential risks in complex agreements
  • Provide negotiation points for better outcomes
  • Help students understand complex legal language
  • Enable small businesses to review contracts without expensive counsel

Built on Google's cutting-edge Gemini 1.5 Flash model, Project Legis represents what's possible when legal expertise meets advanced AI technology. It's not a finished product, but a demonstration of how AI can augment‚Äînot replace‚Äîlegal analysis.
